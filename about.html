About Me

Hello, my name is Anhar Khoirun Najib, called Anhar. I am a 5th semester student majoring in Informatics, Mulawarman University. I come from Sangatta, East Kutai Regency, East Kalimantan Province. I am currently taking part in an independent study, namely bangkit program. I took the cloud computing learning path, and am currently working on a final project basic web programming class. This is an East Kalimantan website which contains all the culture and unique things in East Kalimantan. With this website, it is hoped that it can add insight about East Kalimantan, thank you.
